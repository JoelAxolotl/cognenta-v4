Workflow & Routing
How does the workflow routing work? We build a custom Triage Checkbox directly into your CRM. If the team logs a straightforward deal, they leave it blank. If they log a complex deal, they check the box. We only fire when you need us to.
What if the draft doesn't sound exactly right? Our voice profiling gets the tone 98% perfect out of the gate. Because it lives in the draft folder, your AE retains absolute authority. If a highly complex deal requires a specific nuance, they can add that final 2% human touch in seconds before hitting send.
How quickly will we see results? You will start seeing contextual drafts in your team's queue within 48 hours of onboarding your post-call revenue recovery infrastructure.
Operations & Voice Profiling
How do you actually build my AE's voice profile? We ask for 3 to 5 of your AE's most successful past follow-ups. Our system analyzes their sentence length, greeting style, and vocabulary to build their custom profile.
What happens when we hire new AEs? You simply drop their writing samples into your onboarding folder, and their unique voice profile is live and generating drafts in minutes.
Security & Deliverability
Will this hurt our domain reputation? No. Spam filters are built to catch volume and pattern-matching. Every Cognenta draft is a unique, 1-to-1 email sent manually from your actual outbox like your SDR or AE normally would.
Is there a human reading our prospect notes? No. Cognenta is a 100% automated system. Your data passes securely via API from your CRM straight into your AE’s drafts folder. Meaning only you have access to the data.
How do we know our data is safe? We practice strict data minimization. Most importantly, we do not use your proprietary CRM data or prospect notes to train our AI models.
