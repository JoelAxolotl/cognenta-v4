How Our Managed Protocol Works

We sit directly in the Dead Zone — handling the research, CRM hygiene, and drafting so your Founders and AEs can focus on closing deals.

1. The Single Source of Truth

Your SDR finishes the call and submits a 3-bullet intake form. We instantly push this to HubSpot or Salesforce. Zero double-data entry.

2. Deep Account Enrichment

If the SDR's notes are thin, our internal ops stack fills the gaps — recent funding, verified tech stack data, and industry news via Clay.com.

3. The 15-Minute Bridge Draft

We transform the raw data into a technical bridge email grounded in exactly two sources: your verified product documentation and the prospect's actual context.

4. Human Control, Always

The draft lands in your AE's Slack or Drafts folder, marked Pending Review. One click to review. One click to send. You control 100% of what goes out.
